Falcon 1

By Jon Ross

Manufacturer

  • SpaceX (founded June 2002)

Vehicle Configuration

  • F1 (M1A) Expendable (0 stage)
Also known as F1.

The Falcon 1 is SpaceX's first launch vehicle and is the first privately-developed liquid-fuel launch vehicle to reach orbit. Falcon 1 flights 1 through 3 were failures resulting in loss of payload, while Falcon 1 flight 4 was a success.
